# Week-wise Plan: Increasing the User Base

**Project:** WanderAI — AI Travel Itinerary Planner
**Author:** Aryan Agarwal
**Status:** Hackathon submission — forward-looking growth plan

## 1. Week-wise Outreach Plan

**Goal**: Grow WanderAI's user base from initial hackathon testers to a sustainable, actively-used travel planning tool within 4 weeks of launch.

| Week | Activities & Targets |
|------|------------------------|
| **Week 1** | Share WanderAI with hackathon peers, college WhatsApp/Discord groups, and immediate network for initial testing. Target: 15–20 testers. Collect first round of feedback via the in-app review system (star ratings + written feedback). |
| **Week 2** | Analyse review feedback for common pain points (e.g. itinerary accuracy, INR pricing edge cases, language quality). Fix top 3 reported issues. Add 2–3 most-requested travel styles or destinations based on tester input. |
| **Week 3** | Public outreach: post on LinkedIn and Twitter/X targeting travel and AI/ML communities. Highlight the INR-first pricing and Hindi/Telugu language support as key differentiators versus generic travel planners. Target: 100+ unique visitors. |
| **Week 4** | Partner outreach: contact college travel/exchange program coordinators and student trip-planning groups who plan trips regularly. Offer WanderAI as a free planning tool for their members. Target: 3–5 group partnerships. |

## 2. Continuous Improvement Loop

WanderAI improves continuously through a structured feedback loop:

- **Collect:** In-app review system captures star ratings, destination planned, and free-text feedback from every user.
- **Analyse:** Weekly review of average rating trends and recurring complaints to prioritise fixes.
- **Iterate:** Prompt engineering refinements (e.g. improving INR cost accuracy, adding more granular travel styles) are pushed via the CI/CD pipeline with automated lint, type-check, and test validation before deployment.
- **Re-engage:** Users who left reviews are informed (via outreach channels) when their suggested features ship, encouraging repeat usage and word-of-mouth referrals.

## 3. Feature & Functionality Roadmap

Planned enhancements to increase user retention and stickiness:

- PDF export of generated itineraries for offline use while travelling.
- Save and revisit past itineraries (lightweight session-based history).
- Map view with pinned attractions for each day's plan.
- Additional Indic language support (Tamil, Bengali, Marathi) based on demand signals from reviews.
